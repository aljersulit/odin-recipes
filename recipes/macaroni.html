Macaroni Salad Recipe

Creamy Macaroni Salad: A Refreshing Side Dish

This creamy macaroni salad is a refreshing and delicious side dish, perfect for picnics, barbecues, or as a light lunch. Elbow macaroni is combined with crisp vegetables like celery, bell peppers, and onions, then tossed in a tangy, creamy dressing. The salad is chilled for a few hours to allow the flavors to meld together, making it a great make-ahead dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup carrots, grated
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  3. Add the chopped celery, red bell pepper, red onion, carrots, and sweet pickle relish (if using) to the dressing. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add the cooked macaroni to the bowl and gently toss until the macaroni is evenly coated with the dressing.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the macaroni salad for at least 1-2 hours to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Before serving, give the salad a quick stir and garnish with fresh parsley.
  7. Enjoy this creamy macaroni salad as a side dish with grilled meats or sandwiches!
